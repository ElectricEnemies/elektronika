aestesis module (2D effect)

form and colour generator using audio/video in. can be used to colour a video stream

input/output

1 audio in
1 video in
1 video out

properties

the module is divided into 2 parts. on the left are the source controls and on the right are the colour controls

choose from 3 source types: basic forms, zygo or image (from video in)
use the form selector to choose cross, box, circle or diagonals
 the zygo controls adjust level, speed and depth
the image controls adjust contrast and blur for the image from the video in source

choose from 4 colour types: palettized, 1 channel sound (palettized), 2 channel sound (palettized) and 3 channel sound (rgb)
palette: cycle and 4 band palette colour options
the central 4 buttons control the effect of the sound envelope (expand, smooth, delay and speed)
the 3 dials at the bottom adjust the effect of bass, medium and treble using the colour selected in the boxes
