loop107 module (2D effect)

video sampler and timed replay (4 second buffer)

input/output

1 audio in
1 video in
1 video shape in
1 video out

properties

fade source dial
activate source to fill buffer

select effect 1 to 6 on far right

1. scratch. scratch loop view, loop start position dial, loop length dial
2. cycle. replay at the specified speed, activate ping-pong
3. random. adjust random jump time
4. audio. adjust bass, medium and treble levels
5. shape. adjust fade shape
6. sinus. adjust wave mix and frequency/phase for both waves
