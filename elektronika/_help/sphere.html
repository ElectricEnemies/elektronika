sphere module (2D effect)

deformation, and using audio in for deformation

input/output

1 audio in
4 video in
1 video out

properties

sphere module is composed of 4 areas
source selection, mirror effects, polar deformation and planar deformation

buttons [1/2/3/4], select the video source
adjust master feedback

select cross, diagonal, vertical and horizontal mirrors (and combine!)
position adjuster

polar adjustments: polar master, effect direction, twirl, rotation, circle waves, rosace, zoom and punch-pinch
audio direction, polar/audio wave, audio pulse, audio zoom, audio fly

planar adjustments: planar master, effect direction, E1, E2, E3,
audio direction, S1, S2 and S3
