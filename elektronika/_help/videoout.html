video out module (in/out)

send video stream to video device (window, overlay window, directX driver)

input/output

4 video in
1 video out

properties

choose the video out device from the drop down list
use buttons 1/2/3/4 to select the video in stream

device list

[window] a simple window, can be resized and moved to the second video out (dual head)
[overlay window] can be resized, overlay window has vsync activate clone/zoom mode on your
video drivers display properties and this windows appear automatically in full screen on the second video out
[directX] select a second video device, for full screen video out on the second head
[gdi] netmeeting driver
