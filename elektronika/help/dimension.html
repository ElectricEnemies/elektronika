dimension module (3D effect)

directX 3D effects and direct control over video out

input/output

1 audio in
4 video in

properties

the dimension effect can handle 3 input effects followed by 3 display effects
at the top you can choose the clear colour, clear power and video out
the video out allows you to scale up from small scale clips eg 320x240 to a larger display eg 800x600 taking advantage of your video card's antialiasing power

the dimension effect controls are on the right side of the interface
- power knob
- input effect selection
- effect selection
- blend mode
- color adjust

the default input is video. select the video source or background
the default effect is morph, use this to deform the image
