mimix module (mixer)

mix 4 videos streams using blended mix (fade/add/subtract/multiply and subtract without saturation)

input/output

4 audio in
1 audio out
4 video in
1 video out

properties

adjust fade and fade colour
adjust zoom level and size
adjust blur level and size
for each source adjust level, zoom, luminosity, saturation and hue and
choose fade, add with saturation, subtract with saturation, multiply, add without saturation
and subtract without saturation (like photoshop difference)
master level button on the right
