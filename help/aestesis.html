help file

 

aestesis module
on sound form and color generator can be used to color video stream

inputs/outputs

1 video out
1 video in

usage

divided in two part
on the left, the source controls
on the right, the color controls

3 sources types, basic forms, zygo or image
basic forms: cross, box, circle and diagonals
zygo form controls: level, speed and depth
image (the video in pin) controls: contrast and blur

4 color types, paletized, 1 channel sound (paletized), 2 channels sound (paletized), 3 channels sound (rgb)
palette control, cycle and 4 band palette color
the four buttons just down control the sound enveloppe (expand, smooth, delay and speed)
the 3 color box set the 3 channels sound colors with the level just down
